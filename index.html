Dutch Apple Pie > American Apple Pie

Dutch Apple Pie has a Streseul top. Streseul is way better than plain old crust. Mostly all good Dutch pie shops are really just a cafe with forced outside seating. This is nice, because you typically order inside and you can make things appear like you're ordering for three people, when in reality you just intend to eat three pieces of pie.'
